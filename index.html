About BMW

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) is a German multinational automobile manufacturer founded in 1916. The company is headquartered in Munich, Germany. BMW is globally recognized for producing luxury vehicles, motorcycles, and high-performance cars.

Originally, BMW started as an aircraft engine manufacturer during World War I. Over the decades, the company expanded into automobile production and became one of the world's leading premium car brands.

BMW is known for its strong focus on engineering excellence, innovative technology, and superior driving dynamics. The brand slogan "The Ultimate Driving Machine" reflects its commitment to performance and driver satisfaction.

The company operates under three major brands: BMW, MINI, and Rolls-Royce Motor Cars. Today, BMW continues to lead in electric mobility with its BMW i Series and sustainable automotive solutions.
